2.2.5. Setting or Changing Course Grading Scheme

To change the grade scheme in the program, click the 'Class' option on the toolbar, and then click 'Grade Scheme'. This is illustrated in Figure 1.

Figure 1: Clicking on 'Grade Scheme' from the 'Class' option on the toolbar

A pop up dialog will appear that will let the user change the grading schema.

Figure 2: The dialog box with the options of which grade scheme to choose

There is the option to give 'pass/fail' or a graded scale with letter grades, as illustrated in Figure 2.
